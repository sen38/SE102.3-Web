Description
  • Experience the wonders of Sri Lankan wildlife on a three-hour safari in Kaudulla National Park, situated near the city of Galoya. This park, with its diverse ecosystem, offers excellent opportunities to witness majestic elephants and other creatures in their native habitat.
  • Travel by the reservoir, the lifeblood of the park that attracts herds of elephants from neighboring areas. Alongside elephants, spot Sambar deer, Axis deer, chevrotains, wild boar, Asian spoonbills, painted storks, and more. If luck is on your side, you might even catch a glimpse of leopards or sloth bears in the forest.
  • As you traverse the park, admire the green line of trees, the blue sky, and emerald green grasslands. Breathe in the fresh air and check off an amazing safari experience from your bucket list.
