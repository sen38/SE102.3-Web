Anuradhapura and Mihintale one day trip


Wander through the pagodas, monastic structures, and ponds scattered across the historic ruins of Anuradhapura on a day tour lasting 10 hours from Dambulla. Explore landmarks such as Jetavanarama, Sri Maha Bodhi, and others, enhancing your understanding of Sri Lanka's rich history.

Description
  • Embark on a day tour from Dambulla to witness the ancient city of Anuradhapura's ruins, captivating monuments, and one of the most vital sites in Sri Lankan Buddhism, Mihintale.
  • Commence your day with a convenient morning pick-up from your accommodations in Dambulla, setting off on a 1.5-hour drive to Anuradhapura. Accompanied by your guide, explore ancient monuments, delving into the rich tapestry of Sri Lankan history.
  • Take a break for lunch in the afternoon and then journey to Mihintale, believed to be the birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka. Uncover the historical significance of this crucial site.
  • Conclude your day with a relaxed transfer back to your hotel in Dambulla.
