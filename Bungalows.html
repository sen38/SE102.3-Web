Thotalagala Plantation House 

Thotalagala Plantation House is located in Haputale on the Pitaratmalie Estate. Thotalagala is a restored former planters bungalow featuring seven Suites. The bungalow has a restaurant, garden, bar, pool, and free WiFi throughout the hotel.

For more details www.thotalagala.com/


Nine Skies 

Nine Skies is located in Ella. It features free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool, a garden, and a shared lounge in Ella. Demodara Nine Arch Bridge is 7.8 km from Nine Skies, while Demodara Railway Station is 1.2 km away. For more details www.nine-skies.com

Goatfell Kandapola 

Goatfell is located in Kandapola, Nuwara Eliya. It has free bikes, an outdoor swimming pool, and a garden. Gregory Lake is 15 km from Goatfell, while Hakgala Botanical Garden is 22 km from the bungalow.
For more details www.goatfell.com


Ceylon Tea Trails  

Located in Hatton, 20 km from Adam's Peak, Ceylon Tea Trails offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden, and a terrace. A hot tub and a bicycle rental service are available for guests.For more details www.ceylontea-trails.com

Rosyth Estate House 

Situated in Kegalle, 32 km from Kandy Royal Botanic Gardens, Rosyth Estate House features accommodation with free bikes, free private parking, an outdoor swimming pool, and a garden.

Haggai Tourist Bungalow & Suites 

Haggai Tourist Bungalow is located in Colombo, 2.9 km from Wellawatte Beach and 4.9 km from Bambalapitiya Railway Station. Featuring a restaurant, shared lounge, garden, and free WiFi.For more detailswww.haggai.lk


Gregorys Bungalow 

Gregorys Bugalow is situated just less than 1 km from Gregory Lake, in Nuwara Eliya. It has access to free bikes and a garden.
