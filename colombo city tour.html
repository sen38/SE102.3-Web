Description
  • Dive into the vibrant heart of Sri Lanka's capital, Colombo, and embrace the city's sights, sounds, and aromas with your private guide. Experience the top must-see attractions, uncover hidden gems, and savor local snacks. Board a colorful Tuk Tuk and let your guide whisk you from one highlight to the next. Kickstart your tour at Independence Square, a must-visit and an ideal starting point. Roam the expansive green lawns, marvel at one of the island's oldest colonial buildings, and delve into Sri Lanka's historical past as your host narrates the transformation of the city into its modern state.
  • Embrace local customs by sampling delicious hoppers and vegetable roti, popular morning snacks, especially favored by Colombo's park-goers! Traverse to the city's largest park, a serene haven with lush vegetation, century-old trees, and a White House replica. Tuk Tuk your way to the oldest Buddhist temples with a scenic lake, home to a remarkable collection of Buddha statues, a holy tree, and a relic of Buddha's hair. Your host is ready to answer any questions that pique your curiosity!
  • Immerse yourself in authentic local life with a visit to one of the liveliest markets, where locals trade everything from vegetables to electronics. Meander through labyrinthine streets, follow your nose to a cassava chip vendor, and witness the preparation of this delectable snack. Head to the banana market for a diverse banana tasting experience. Curious for more? Explore a mosque shaped like a pomegranate, a minimalist Hindu temple, and Sri Lanka's last King's jail cell, complemented by a breathtaking view from the lighthouse. Conclude your tour at the tranquil ocean side of Galle Face, where you can unwind with a cold drink, savor a delicious prawn cake, and witness a splendid sunset over the western coast of Sri Lanka.
