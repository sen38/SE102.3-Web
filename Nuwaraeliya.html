Nuwara Eliya one day trip 


Embark on a journey to Little England and uncover a treasure trove of enchanting attractions. Delight in the serene beauty of the Hanuman Temple, wander through lush Tea Plantations, marvel at the cascading Ramboda Waterfalls, enjoy the tranquility of Gregory Lake, and immerse yourself in the scenic charm of Victoria Park.

Description
  • Commence your exploration with a 7:30 am pick-up, setting out for Nuwara Eliya. Pause in Ramboda to witness the awe-inspiring Ramboda Waterfalls and the sacred Hanuman Temple, followed by an insightful visit to a Tea Plantation and Tea Factory.
  • Upon reaching Nuwara Eliya in time for lunch, savor a delightful meal at the Summer Hill Breeze Hotel. Embark on a city tour of Nuwara Eliya, taking in the scenic allure of Gregory Lake and the serene ambiance of Victoria Park. Nestled amidst hills and tea plantations, Nuwara Eliya boasts year-round spring-like weather. Once a favored retreat during the colonial era, the town is adorned with English country-style houses and charming half-timbered bungalows.
  • Around 4:00 pm, bid farewell to Nuwara Eliya and return to your Kandy hotel.
