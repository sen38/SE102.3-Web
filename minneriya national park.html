Jeep Safari to Minneriya National Park


Journey to Minneriya National Park to witness a significant congregation of Asian elephants. Additionally, observe a diverse array of rare bird species, as well as amphibians and reptiles, some of which are exclusive to this area.

Description
  • Commence your adventure with an afternoon pickup from your hotel or residence, heading to Minneriya National Park to embark on the safari.
  • Minneriya National Park spans about 21,000 acres (8,889 hectares) and houses a diverse array of wildlife species. Seize the opportunity to observe the large herds of magnificent Sri Lankan elephants in their natural habitat up close. Look out for two rare species of monkeys endemic to Sri Lanka, namely the Purple-faced Langur Monkey and the Toque Macaque. The park is also home to rare and endangered species such as the Sri Lankan leopard and Sri Lankan sloth bear, along with saltwater crocodiles and Indian pythons, which you may be fortunate enough to encounter.
  • After a 3-hour safari spotting these various rare animals, your transport will return you to your accommodation.
