Description
  • Anuradhapura, declared a UNESCO world heritage city, stands as Sri Lanka's inaugural known kingdom and served as the island's capital for numerous centuries. Recognized for its cultural and religious significance, the city beckons both local and international visitors fascinated by Sri Lanka's ancient history.
  • Witness the remnants of the Brazen Palace, a once towering 9-story structure, and the Sri Maha Bodhi, a Bo Tree aged over 2000 years, grown from the very branch beneath which the Buddha attained enlightenment. Explore the ruins of the Ruwanweliseya Dagaba, witness the 2nd-century shrine housing the Buddha's collarbone relic, and marvel at the Abhayagiriya temple and Jetawanaramaya dagobas.
