Experience

Highlights
  • Explore a turtle hatchery and touch baby turtles, participating in their release into the sea.
  • Enjoy a Madu Ganga river boat safari to discover local wildlife.
  • Witness stilt fishermen at work.
Description
  • Clients will be picked up from Colombo and transferred to Bentota, where they have the opportunity to engage in water sports at Bentota River, such as Jet-ski and Banana boat, at their own expense. This river serves as the boundary between the Western Province and the Southern Province. The journey continues to a Turtle Hatchery, where clients can touch baby turtles and participate in releasing them into the sea.
  • After the turtle experience, the tour proceeds to enjoy a Madu Ganga river boat safari. This safari includes exploration of extinct animals, fish, aquatic plants, and picturesque mangroves. Clients can also indulge in a fish foot massage in the middle of the river
  • Next, the tour visits Mithiyagoda Moonstone Mine, providing an opportunity to witness the authentic mining process of Moonstone. Clients can also purchase genuine Moonstone at a discounted price as it is sourced directly from the factory. Mithiyagoda is a unique location in Sri Lanka as one of the few places in the world where Moonstone is found.
  • The tour then proceeds to lunch at Hikkaduwa at a beachside restaurant, where clients can enjoy seafood at their own expense. If clients are interested, they have the option to engage in snorkeling or scuba diving to explore sea corals and fish.
  • Following the water activities, the tour departs to Galle Dutch Fort, a UNESCO heritage site, to explore its various attractions. Clients can enjoy stunning views of Galle Dutch Fort and the sunset. Afterward, they will be returned to Colombo, and drop-off will be at the same pick-up location.
