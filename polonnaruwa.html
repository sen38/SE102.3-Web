Description
  • Embark on a captivating day tour from Colombo to Polonnaruwa, delving into the rich historical tapestry of Sri Lanka as you traverse the UNESCO-listed ancient city. Marvel at intricate statues, stupas, and ancient avenues, and witness the regal palace that echoes with history. Admire the awe-inspiring rock-cut Buddhas at Gal Vihara and soak in the grandeur of the Parakrama Samudra reservoir. Expert guides illuminate the heritage of Polonnaruwa, turning this journey into an unforgettable experience of cultural exploration and architectural marvels.
