Wildlife Safari to Yala Nationa Park


Embark on a private day tour from Hikkaduwa and explore Yala National Park through a Jeep safari. Witness the elusive leopards, observe herds of elephants, and gain insights into the diverse array of wildlife inhabiting this region.

Description
  • Embark on a Private Jeep Safari Tour from Hikkaduwa to uncover the treasures of Yala National Park, the second-largest wildlife park in Sri Lanka. Experience the untouched splendor of nature and encounter incredible wildlife.
  • Commence your adventure with a convenient hotel pickup in Hikkaduwa. Relax as you journey to Yala National Park, where a Jeep safari awaits. Keep your eyes peeled for local leopards and elephants in their natural surroundings.
  • Encounter other native animals like sloth bears, jackals, mongoose, spotted deer, buffalo, wild boars, sambhur, and hare. Capture memorable moments with your camera as you navigate through their natural habitats.
  • Witness a vibrant array of butterflies and diverse bird species. After the safari, return to your accommodation in Hikkaduwa.
