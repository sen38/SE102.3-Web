Sinharaja Rain Forest one day hike


Embark on this unique one-day hike to Sinharaja Rainforest, immersing yourself in the untouched beauty and ecological wonders of one of Sri Lanka's most prized natural treasures. Book your adventure now and let the rainforest reveal its secrets to you!

Experience

Highlights
  • Guided Hike: Join our knowledgeable guides who will lead you through the enchanting trails, providing insights into the rich ecological significance of Sinharaja.
  • Biodiversity Exploration: Witness a stunning array of plant and animal species unique to this rainforest, as you traverse its diverse landscapes and discover the hidden gems within.
  • Waterfalls and Streams: Encounter the serene beauty of cascading waterfalls and crystal-clear streams, creating picturesque scenes amidst the verdant surroundings.
Description
  • Tour starts from the Kurulugala entrance. Immerse yourself in the rejuvenating embrace of nature as you discover the cascading beauty of Degaldoruwa waterfall. Here, the crystal-clear waters beckon, inviting you to indulge in a refreshing bath and create memories amidst the pristine surroundings
  • Continue your odyssey to the captivating Twins waterfalls, where nature's symphony unfolds in a harmonious dance of water and foliage. Revel in the twin cascades and, if you wish, partake in the invigorating experience of bathing in this natural oasis.
  • Traverse the vast and picturesque Southern Plains, where panoramic views stretch as far as the eye can see. Marvel at the undulating landscapes, lush greenery, and the serenity that defines this pristine region.
  • Ascend to the summit of Kurulugala mountain, where a panoramic vista awaits. Gaze upon the sprawling landscapes below, capturing the essence of Sri Lanka's natural beauty from this elevated vantage point.
