Bambarakanda 

Explore Bambarakanda Falls, Sri Lanka's highest waterfall at 263 meters (863 feet). Nestled in the Badulla District, the trek through tea plantations leads to this awe-inspiring natural wonder. A must-see for nature lovers and adventurers, it promises an unforgettable experience amidst lush greenery and stunning landscapes.
