Bungalows  

Bungalows are charming single-story dwellings known for their cozy, intimate atmospheres and often feature spacious verandas or surrounding outdoor spaces. These quaint residences offer a sense of seclusion and tranquility, making them popular choices for vacation retreats or as peaceful residential abodes.

Camping Sites 

Camping sites provide nature enthusiasts with a rustic and immersive outdoor experience, offering a chance to connect with the natural environment. Equipped with basic amenities such as tent spaces, fire pits, and sometimes communal facilities, these sites offer an adventurous escape for those seeking simplicity and a break from modern comforts.
