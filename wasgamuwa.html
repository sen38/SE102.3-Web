Description
  • Adjacent to the historic city of Polonnaruwa, Wasgamuwa National Park stands as one of Sri Lanka's precious natural treasures. During a morning or afternoon 3-hour safari, delve into the richness of the flora and fauna within the dry tropical forest, enjoying a private safari ride amid the refreshing air and the symphony of crickets and birds echoing through the landscape.
  • Rest assured that this safari will offer glimpses of majestic elephants, a prominent feature of the national park. The park is also home to a diverse array of mammals, including leopards, sloth bears, purple-faced langur monkeys, wild boars, sambar deer, spotted deer, buffalo, and the endemic yellow-fronted barbet.
  • Throughout the national park, small water pools serve as rich habitats for various aquatic life, including water monitors, crocodiles, pythons, and a variety of beautiful aquatic birds. Wildlife enthusiasts can expect clear views of the fauna surrounding these pools, presenting ample opportunities to capture extraordinary photographs.
