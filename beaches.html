Nilaweli 

Situated on the northeastern coast of Sri Lanka near Trincomalee, is renowned for its pristine white sands, clear turquoise waters, and a serene tropical atmosphere. It's a tranquil escape, perfect for relaxation, water activities, and exploring nearby coral reefs.
